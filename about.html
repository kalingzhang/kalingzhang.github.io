Kaling is a multi-disciplinary designer based in Toronto, working in film and television.

She works across graphics, set design, and concept renderings, creating story-driven work with a thoughtful yet playful approach.

Before film, she spent several years at creative agencies and architecture firms. That experience shaped how she thinks about space, image, and design under pressure.
